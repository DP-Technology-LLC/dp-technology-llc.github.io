Maint

About Maint

Maint is short for maintenance. Users can see useful statistics regarding the data that RCC is currently storing. You can also access the Settings for RCC by tapping on the three dots in the upper right portion of the display.

Properites of a Maint

There are no properties within the Maint view itself. You can view information on RCC's settings here.

Tasks/Functions

There are no tasks/functions within the Maint view itself. It just displays useful statistics on RCC's data.
