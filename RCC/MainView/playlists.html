Playlists

About Playlists

Playlists allow users to group Playable Items together. When a Playlist is created a user can start with one item in the list and RCC will move onto the next one. Users can also have RCC pick a random Playable Item to play.

Properites of a Playlist

  • Name
    • A free text entry for you to give your Playlist a name. It is not a unique field so if you are the type who would name all your kids or pets the same name, go for it we won't stop you here.
  • Playable Items
    • A list that displays the Playable Items you added to the Playlist. Tap on one to remove from the list, to add go back to the list of Playlists and you can add from there.

Tasks/Functions

From the Playlist list there are a number of tasks/functions you do

  • Play
    • Play will create a Play Playlist Task and attempt to play your Playlist on the device that you choose.
  • Play Random Item
    • Play Random will create a Launch Task and attempt to play a random Playable Item within the Playlist on the device that you choose.
  • Add Playable Item
    • This is how you populate your Playlist. You will be shown the same list of Playable Items that you currently see under Playable Items.
