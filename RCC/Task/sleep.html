Sleep Task

About Sleep Task

The Sleep Task is a way to mimic what you may see on a standard television with some additional niceties. You can use it to simply send a list of commands after a certain time or you can have it turn the volume down as time goes by. This task is great for people who have a Roku Streambar connected to a computer monitor.

Task Settings

  • Notches
    • A free-text entry for how many times you want RCC to turn the volume down.

  • Minutes Per Cycle
    • A free-text entry for how long to wait to turn the volume down.

  • Cycles
    • A free-text entry for how many times to turn the volume down.

  • Afterwards
    • A free-text entry for the commands you want to run. You can also use the Command List Builder to ensure you create valid commands by tapping on the button next to the entry. Generally you would want to provide the 'PowerOff' or 'Home' command.

  • Name, Push Notification upon completion and Start Time
    • See common RCC task properites here.

FAQs

  • Notches, Minutes per Cycle and Cycles; huh?
    • Say you want to turn down the volume two times every 10 minutes and then in 30 minutes, go to the home screen. You would have Notches = 2, Minutes per Cycle = 10, Cycles = 3 and the Afterwards command = 'Home'.
