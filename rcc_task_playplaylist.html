Play Playlist Task

About Play Playlist Task

The Play Playlist Task plays Playable Items that you have added to a Playlist. Once the task is started RCC will monitor your device and when it is not streaming, RCC will launch the next Playable Item. If you configure the task to Move to Next after content changes, RCC will try determine when you are done watching your current content and move to the next.

Task Settings

  • Repeat
    • A Checkbox setting indicating if RCC should play the first item when the last one is finished. If unchecked the task will end when the last item finishes.

  • Move to Next after content changes
    • A Checkbox setting indicating if RCC will take additional measures to determine if your Playable Item is finished. If unchecked RCC will only move to next item when your Device is done streaming. So if your Playable Item is a series in a channel that auto-plays the next episode, the channel will continue to the next episode and RCC will not attempt to play the next Playable Item.

  • Start Time

FAQs

  • Do I have to start with the first Playable Item in my Playlist?
    • No. When you tap Play on your Playlist, you can select which Playable Item to start with in the details view.

  • Now that I started the Playlist can I start watching a different Playable Item in the list?
    • Yes! Under Running Tasks, tap on your Play Playlist Task and then tap View Details. In the details, select the desired Playable Item and tap Play.
