Quick Launch Widget

About Quick Launch Widget

The Quick Launch Widget is a feature that allows you to play a pre-determined Playable Item on a pre-determined Device. You can place this widget on your home screen and when you tap it RCC will attempt to play your Playable Item on your Device.

FAQs

  • Where is this widget located?
    • This may vary on your device. Try tapping and holding an empty spot on your home screen. You may see "Widgets" as an option. Look for the "RCC Quick Launch" widget. Once located tap and hold and you should be able to place it on your home screen.
  • I got the widget on my home screen but it says "No Playable Item", "No Device" and "Set values in RCC Settings."
    • For the widget to work you need to assign an existing Playable Item and an existing Device. This can be found in RCC's settings (Maint -> Settings). The two settings should be near the bottom and labeled "Quick Launch - Playable Item" and "Quick Launch - Device".
  • I assigned both a Playable Item and Device but the widget still says "No Playable Item" and "No Device"
    • At this time the Quick Launch Widget does not auto update. Simply tap on the widget and the text should be updated. You can then tap again to tell RCC to play your Playable Item on your Device.
